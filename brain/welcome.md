# Welcome to BC🖤CORTEX

This folder (`brain/`) is your mind. Every `.md` file in here becomes a **star** on
the map. Drop your own notes in, then run `bash run.sh` to rebuild.

These sample notes are just a demo — delete them and add your own.

- New here? Read [[getting-started]].
- Curious what this is? See [[about-bc-cortex]].

Subfolders become **color groups** (this file is in the top level; `notes/` and
`ideas/` each get their own color). A folder named `private/` stays hidden until
you unlock it with your passphrase.
